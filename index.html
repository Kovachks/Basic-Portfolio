About Me

Once I graduated in 2013 I decided to follow my girlfriend to the carolinas and moved to Charlotte, NC. I have lived here ever since and love it down here.

My hobbies include video games, which I mostly play World of Warcraft, Overwatch, Squad and a few select others depending on how I'm feeling. I love sports and am an avid basketball player along with following my favorite teams which include the Cleveland Cavaliers, Cleveland Indians and all Cincinnati Bearcats athletic teams
